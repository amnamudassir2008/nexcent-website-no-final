Hair Fall

Hair fall is a common symptom of PCOS caused by hormonal imbalance. Increased male hormones can weaken hair roots, leading to thinning hair and excessive hair loss, especially from the scalp

Weight Gain

Weight gain is another common symptom of PCOS. Hormonal imbalance and insulin resistance can make it difficult to maintain a healthy weight, even with a normal diet and routine.

Irregular or Missed Periods

Irregular or missed periods are one of the main symptoms of PCOS. Due to hormonal imbalance, ovulation may not occur regularly, which can cause delayed, irregular, or absent menstrual cycles.
